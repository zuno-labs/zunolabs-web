Privacy & Terms

Straightforward. Your data belongs to you.

// Last updated: March 2026 · Nomly v1.0

Who we are

Nomly is developed by ZunoLabs. Questions? zunolabs2025@gmail.com

What we collect

  • Account info — your email address, used for authentication via Supabase.
  • Food logs — meals you record: place, dish, rating, optional notes and photo.
  • Location — used to show your position on the map and find nearby places. We do not store continuous location history.
  • Analytics — anonymized usage events via PostHog. No personal data attached.

How we use your data

  • To sync your food logs across devices.
  • To generate AI insights about your food history (Nomly Pro only) — processed server-side via Google Gemini. Your data is not stored by Google.
  • To process subscriptions via RevenueCat and Google Play. We never store payment information.

Third-party services

  • Supabase — authentication and database (hosted on AWS).
  • Google Places API — place search and location data.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management.
  • PostHog — anonymized product analytics.
  • Google Gemini — AI insight generation (Pro only, server-side).

Security

All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS. We use row-level security (RLS) so users can only access their own data. Logs are also cached locally on-device using SQLite.

Your rights

Delete your account and all data at any time via Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your profile, logs, and cached insights from our servers.

Children's privacy

Nomly is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We'll notify you of significant changes via email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
